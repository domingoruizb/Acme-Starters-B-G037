This is a  DP2 project developed by group G037 during the academic year 2025/2026.  It is intended to be a core project 
for the students who have enrolled the Design and Testing subject of the Software Engineering curriculum of the 
University of Seville.  The goal of this project is to develop a web information system that helps Acme Starters, Inc.
manage their business. Acme Starters, Inc. is a fictitious company that specialises in helping entrepreneurs publicise
their ideas.
